2Truths&AI

2Truths&AI is a web game that blends the classic game "two truths and a lie" and artificial intelligence. Each round presents three statements —— two are true, one is a lie, and all are AI.

Your task is to spot the fake.

This, along with the three prints (avail for sale, hmu), is designed to make us think about how much of what is generated by AI... is a lie.
